In Wrestle Online, it's time to earn the cup! Get ready to face strong opponents, and be the one to win each match. Different from classic wrestling, the one with the strongest legs wins the competition. Do you have what it takes to ground your opponents?

You can hear the crowd cheering your name, so you better don't disappoint your fans. Your opponent is waiting for you, so get ready! The objective of this game is to win each match by scoring 5 stars. The game features 3 different modes. The first one is the solo mode where you fight against the CPU. The second mode is the 2 player mode, where you can play against each other with a friend by sharing the same keyboard. Lastly, there is the multiplayer mode. In this game mode, you can play against other players online. Keep pressing on the jump button to make your wrestler keep jumping and make your opponent's back touch the ground. You can earn coins as you play. With these, you can unlock 10 different character skins to use. Have fun!

Challenge a friend and show them you're the best by beating them in our other fun and addictive 2 player games. Enjoy playing!

Controls

Player 1 can use the W key to jump, while player 2 can use the up arrow key to jump.
